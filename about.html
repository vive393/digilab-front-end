Who are we ?

Welcome to DigiLab, your number one source for all digital online solutions. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of online experience, with a focus on dependability, customer service and uniqueness. Founded in 1998 by Vivek, has come a long way from its beginnings in a home office. When Vivek first started out, his passion for providing the best equipment for his fellowemployees drove him to intense research, and gave him the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming online store. We now serve customers all over the US, the Austin area and are thrilled to be a part of the eco-friendly wing of the IT industry. We hope you enjoy our services as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
